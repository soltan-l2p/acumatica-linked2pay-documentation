Troubleshooting

Use a controlled diagnostic sequence, error matrix, logs, transaction history, and escalation criteria.

First-response sequence

  1. Stop retrying until prior outcome is known.
  2. Capture screen ID, document/reference, timestamp/time zone, rail, and sanitized error.
  3. Review Payment Status, Last Status, history, CCProcTran for AR, and portal.
  4. Review Acumatica Trace and App_Data/L2PLogs/webhook_yyyy-MM-dd.log.
  5. Correct data/configuration and run one controlled retry.

When to contact support

Escalate persistent authentication failure, portal/Acumatica disagreement, accepted transactions without tracking, or duplicate-funds risk. Provide sanitized build, screen, document, timestamp, transaction ID, and error. Never provide keys, full tokens, card/bank data, or customer personal information.
