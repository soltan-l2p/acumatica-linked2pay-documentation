Troubleshooting

Use a controlled diagnostic sequence, error matrix, logs, transaction history, and escalation criteria.

First-response sequence

  1. Stop retrying until the prior gateway outcome is known.
  2. Capture screen ID, document/reference, timestamp/time zone, rail, and sanitized error.
  3. Review Payment Status, request state, Webhook History, approved CCProcTran rows, and the linked2pay portal.
  4. Review Acumatica Trace and, when enabled, App_Data/L2PLogs/webhook_yyyy-MM-dd.log.
  5. Correct data or configuration, then run one controlled retry or release-recovery action.

When to contact support

Escalate persistent authentication failure, portal/Acumatica disagreement, unmatched accepted transactions, or duplicate-funds risk. Provide sanitized version, environment, screen, document, timestamp, transaction ID, and error. Never provide keys, full tokens, card/bank data, or customer personal information.
