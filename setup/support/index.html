Support and Escalation

Route implementation, Acumatica, gateway, and security issues with evidence needed for fast diagnosis and no sensitive-data exposure.

Support contacts

NeedContactUse
Implementation/commercialsales@linked2pay.comDeployment, account enablement, demo, or routing.

Gateway/APIsupport@linked2pay.comAuthentication, merchant, transaction, status, webhook service.
Security incidentCustomer security owner and support@linked2pay.comCredential, token, data, or unauthorized webhook exposure.

Severity and escalation

  • Critical: duplicate-funds risk, exposed secrets/payment data, broad outage, high-value unreconciled transaction. Stop and escalate.
  • High: production rail unavailable, broad webhook failure.
  • Normal: single-record setup/data issue, documentation question, sandbox defect.

What to include

  • Acumatica/integration builds.
  • Environment, company/branch, screen ID, rail.
  • Document type/reference and timestamp/time zone.
  • Transaction/payment IDs.
  • Sanitized error/log excerpt.
  • Steps attempted and whether funds moved.
Never attach: API/webhook keys, passwords, full tokens, card/bank data, CVV, production exports, or unmasked customer information.

Before opening a case

Check Acumatica, portal, Payment Status, Webhook History, Trace, and App_Data/L2PLogs. If outcome is unknown, do not retry until history confirms whether funds moved.
