Known Issues

Review validated limitations, mitigations, and production risks before scoping, testing, or upgrading the integration.

Current known issues and limitations

AreaCurrent boundaryRequired approach
VersionOnly Acumatica 2025 R2 build 25.201.0213 is identified as validated.Regression test every other build or customization combination.
Vendor paymentsAccounts Payable payments are not supported in the current release.Scope and demonstrate AR customer collections only.
Customer pullCreating Acumatica customers by pulling all contacts from linked2pay is not implemented or shown.Create customers in Acumatica, then use Sync individually or through linked2pay Center.
Invoice sync/email linksInvoices are not mirrored to linked2pay and invoice-email-link fields are dormant.Use Acumatica invoices and linked2pay payment processing only.
Card brandWallet responses may not contain a reliable brand.Do not infer Visa or another brand; use the configured commercial fallback.
Level 3 eligibilityPartial applications or missing line-level commercial data cannot produce a complete Level 3 payload.Allow fallback and review Trace evidence.
Chargebacks/ACH returnsStatus can be tracked, but accounting reversal documents are not automatic.Define an approved manual accounting procedure.
Gateway-originated void/refundWebhook records an external status but does not silently create Acumatica financial documents.Review the warning and complete the matching Acumatica action.
Fee setupCCFEE is not auto-created.Create and approve the non-stock item before enabling card fees.

Recently resolved behavior

  • API Base URL is configurable per processing center.
  • Initial payment requests use duplicate-prevention and ambiguous-result reconciliation controls.
  • Webhooks are authenticated, idempotent, normalized, retained, and retry unmatched events.
  • Customer Sync creates or recreates missing contacts and mirrors wallet changes by Payment Profile ID.
  • Auto-Bill exposes captured-but-unreleased payments and retries release without recapturing.

Report a new issue

Capture sanitized steps, versions, environment/Base URL, screen ID, document/reference, timestamps, expected/actual results, transaction ID, and logs. Review the support guide, then email support@linked2pay.com.
