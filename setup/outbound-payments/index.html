Future capability

Vendor Payments Roadmap

Vendor-payment processing is planned for a future linked2pay for Acumatica release and is not available today.

Current availability

Not available in the current release. linked2pay for Acumatica currently supports receiving customer credit card and ACH payments through Accounts Receivable. It does not currently send vendor payments.

Roadmap guidance

Vendor payments are planned for a future release. Until linked2pay publishes a supported version and complete workflow, do not include Accounts Payable payments in proposals, demonstrations, implementation scope, configuration, testing, or go-live plans.

What to implement today

Use the documented customer-payment workflows for credit cards, ACH debit, recurring billing, synchronization, fees, refunds, voids, webhooks, and reconciliation.
