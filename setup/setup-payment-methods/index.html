Setup 3A

Create the Credit Card Payment Method

Create the Acumatica payment method used by linked2pay customer card transactions in Accounts Receivable.

Purpose

Create a searchable, active card payment method with the correct usage flags, means of payment, and cash-account availability.

Prerequisites

  • L2PCC processing center active/tested.
  • Approved ID/name and customer card scope.
  • Card cash account available.
  • Payment Methods maintenance rights.

Acumatica navigation path and screen ID

Navigation: Banking > Preferences > Payment Methods list, then open new record (CA2040PL / CA204000)

Step-by-step instructions

In Acumatica, go to Banking > Preferences > Payment Methods (Screen ID: CA2040PL).

This screen is where you confirm the payment methods linked2pay will use for credit card and ACH payments.

Credit card payment method

On the Payment Methods page, click + (New Record) to create a new payment method.

Enter the values for the credit card payment method:

  • Payment Method ID: L2PCC
  • Active: Checked
  • Means of Payment: Credit Card
  • Description: linked2pay Credit Card Payments

Also confirm the following options are selected:

  • Use in AP: Checked
  • Use in AR: Checked
  • Require Remittance Information for Cash Account: Checked

Add your cash account to the payment method. This is the account that will receive credit card payments.

  • Use in AP: Checked
  • Use in AR: Checked

Product availability note

Important: the preserved tutorial shows Use in AP checked. Keep the tutorial configuration as authored, but do not interpret this flag as vendor-payment support. The current release does not send vendor payments.

Expected result

An active L2PCC payment method is available to the intended AR users and cash account.

Validation steps

  • Method appears in AR Payment selector.
  • The tutorial values save successfully; vendor-payment processing remains unavailable.
  • Cash account is selectable and correct.
  • No unrelated cash accounts are enabled.
