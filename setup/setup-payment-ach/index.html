Setup 3C

Create and Link the ACH Payment Method

Create the Acumatica method for tokenized ACH and connect it to L2PACH.

Purpose

Configure ACH as a separately named linked2pay payment method while retaining Acumatica Credit Card means-of-payment behavior required by the processor framework.

Prerequisites

  • L2PACH center tested.
  • Approved ACH cash account.
  • Customer ACH scope approved.
  • Payment Method rights.

Acumatica navigation path and screen ID

Navigation: Banking > Preferences > Payment Methods (CA2040PL / CA204000)

Step-by-step instructions

ACH payment method

For the ACH/bank payment method, follow the same steps used for the credit card payment method. Use these differences:

  • Payment Method ID: L2PACH
  • Means of Payment: Credit Card (This is intentional. Select Credit Card even for ACH payments.)
  • Description: linked2pay ACH/Bank Payments

Expected result

An active L2PACH method is available for intended customer ACH workflows, resolves to L2PACH, and uses the approved ACH cash account.

Validation steps

  • Method appears for intended AR users.
  • L2PACH processing center persists and the approved cash account is mapped.
  • Means of Payment remains Credit Card intentionally.
  • After sync, CCPID and PAYPROFILE match the bank-account ID returned by linked2pay; CONPROFILE matches the customer Contact Profile ID.
  • Only masked account information is displayed and no card fee is calculated.
