Initial Setup

This is the big-picture setup path. Start here, then move through the three foundation steps in order.

How To Use This Section

Follow the setup in order, or jump to the exact step you need.

The setup flow installs the Linked2Pay customization, creates the processing center, and confirms the payment methods Acumatica will use.

Each setup step has its own page in the sidebar. The tutorial-style customer, sync, auto-bill, and webhook steps can stay outside this setup path.

01 Confirm the Extension Is Installed First, make sure the Linked2Pay customization project is published in Acumatica. 02 Create the Linked2Pay Processing Center Next, create the processing center that stores your Linked2Pay connection settings. 03 Create or Confirm Payment Methods Finally, confirm the credit card and ACH payment methods Acumatica will use with Linked2Pay.
Final result: Acumatica has the Linked2Pay extension published, the Linked2Pay processing center is ready, and the payment methods are prepared for the rest of the workflow.
