Initial Setup

This is the big-picture setup path. Start here, then jump into each focused step from the cards below.

How To Use This Section

Follow the setup in order, or jump to the exact step you need.

The setup flow connects Acumatica to Linked2Pay, prepares payment methods, links customers, syncs saved payment profiles, and optionally enables automatic billing.

Each setup step has its own page in the sidebar. The Setup menu opens and closes, so the full checklist is available without crowding the rest of the documentation.

01 Confirm The Extension Is Installed First, make sure the Linked2Pay customization project is published in Acumatica. 02 Create Or Confirm Payment Methods Linked2Pay uses Acumatica payment methods to know whether a payment is a credit card or ACH/bank payment. 03 Create The Linked2Pay Processing Center Next, create the processing center that stores your Linked2Pay connection settings. 04 Attach Payment Methods To The Processing Center Inside the Linked2Pay processing center, open the payment methods section. 05 Test The Credentials After entering the Linked2Pay settings, click the Test Credentials button. 06 Set Up A Customer Now open a customer in Acumatica. 07 Board The Customer To Linked2Pay If the customer does not already exist in Linked2Pay, click Board to Gateway. 08 Sync With Linked2Pay If the customer already exists in Linked2Pay, enter the Linked2Pay Contact ID in Acumatica and click Sync with Linked2Pay. 09 Review Customer Payment Methods After syncing, check the customer payment methods. 10 Enable Auto Bill If Needed If this customer should be charged automatically for due invoices, turn on Enable Auto Bill. 11 Run Linked2Pay Auto-Bill Processing Go to the Linked2Pay Auto-Bill Processing screen. 12 Optional: Schedule Automation If you want the process to run automatically, Acumatica can schedule processing screens. 13 Webhook Setup Linked2Pay can also send payment status updates back to Acumatica.
Final result: customers are connected to Linked2Pay, payment methods are synced into Acumatica, invoices are created in Acumatica, Linked2Pay processes payment, and Acumatica keeps the payment record connected to the invoice.
