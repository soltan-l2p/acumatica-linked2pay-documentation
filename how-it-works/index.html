Implementation

A controlled path from discovery to production

linked2pay installs as a standard Acumatica customization project. No custom development, no middleware, no separate portal - every step is documented screen by screen.

  1. 1

    Import the extension

    Load and publish the linked2pay customization project in your Acumatica instance. No custom development required.

  2. 2

    Connect your account

    Create the processing centers and drop in your linked2pay API credentials and webhook - guided screen by screen in our setup docs.

  3. 3

    Validate payment workflows

    Configure the credit card and ACH payment methods, board a customer, and collect. Your AR workflow stays exactly where it was - inside Acumatica.
