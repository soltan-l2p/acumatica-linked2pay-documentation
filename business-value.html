Business Value

What This Solves

Businesses using Acumatica often collect payments from customers repeatedly. Linked2Pay turns that work into a guided, repeatable, and more automated workflow.

Guided Walkthrough

Show why the workflow matters.

This page helps you explain the business reason before the technical details. The point is not just that the integration exists, but that it removes repeated manual payment work.

Walk the reader through the cards first, then use the checklist to connect the value back to everyday finance operations.

01

Less manual work

Users do not need to manually copy payment tokens, chase payment status, or process each recurring invoice one by one.

02

Cleaner customer records

Each Acumatica customer can store the Linked2Pay Contact ID and synced payment profile references in a consistent location.

03

Automated collections

Auto-Bill can process due invoices for customers with saved Linked2Pay payment methods and the proper customer settings.

Operational Impact

Designed for repeatable payment operations.

  • Reduce re-entry between Acumatica and Linked2Pay.
  • Keep full payment credentials out of Acumatica.
  • Support both card and ACH payment profile workflows.
  • Give staff a cleaner demo and support story.
