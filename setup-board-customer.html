Setup Step 7

Board The Customer To Linked2Pay

If the customer does not already exist in Linked2Pay, click Board to Gateway.

Where To Go

Open this screen first.

In Acumatica, go to Customer Screen > Board to Gateway.

This creates the customer/contact in Linked2Pay and saves the Linked2Pay Contact ID back into Acumatica.

The Linked2Pay Contact ID is important because it connects the Acumatica customer to the customer profile in Linked2Pay.
