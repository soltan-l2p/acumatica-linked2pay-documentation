Suggested Demo Script

This flow is designed for showing the full customer story: setup once, then automate monthly collections.

Guided Walkthrough

Use this as the presenter path.

This page is the live walkthrough. Follow it from top to bottom and you will have a natural story: setup, customer, wallet, invoice, auto-bill, result.

The goal is to make the demo feel smooth, not rushed. The screenshot helps reset the audience before the step list begins.

1

Show processing center

Open the Linked2Pay processing center, show API settings, card fee, payment method mapping, and Test Credentials.

2

Open customer

Show the Linked2Pay Integration tab with Contact ID, Boarded status, and Enable Auto Bill.

3

Board or sync customer

Use Board to Gateway for a new customer, or Sync with Linked2Pay for an existing Linked2Pay contact.

4

Sync payment methods

Show card and ACH payment profiles appearing in Acumatica as masked customer payment methods.

5

Create due invoice

Create or generate an AR invoice for the customer and confirm it is due.

6

Run Auto-Bill

Open Linked2Pay Auto-Bill Processing, select eligible invoices, and process them.

7

Show result

Open the payment record, show invoice application, payment status, and any Linked2Pay transaction details.
