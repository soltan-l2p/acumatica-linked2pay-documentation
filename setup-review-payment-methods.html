Setup Step 9

Review Customer Payment Methods

After syncing, check the customer payment methods.

Where To Go

Open this screen first.

In Acumatica, go to Customer Screen > Payment Methods.

You should see the synced Linked2Pay payment methods. For credit cards, you may see the masked card number and expiration date. For ACH, you may see masked bank account information.

Each payment method stores the Linked2Pay Payment Profile ID. This is the token Acumatica uses when charging that customer through Linked2Pay.
