Invoice

Pay with Card

Use a card payment method to pay the invoice through the linked2pay workflow.

In the last step you released the invoice and created a balance. Now you should be able to see Pay button in the invoice workspace. Click it to start the payment flow.

You should be able to see the payment details page like this:

Attention

Because I picked linked2pay Credit Card as the payment method, you can see there is a Processing Fee added to the total amount.

Note: The processing fee is only for credit card payments. ACH payments do not have a processing fee.

Check Processing Fee to see how you can manage these fees.

You can click on Remove Processing Fee if you want to make exceptions for your customers or exclude processing fee for specific invoices.

And visa versa, you can click on Apply Fee if you want to add after removing it for some reason.

Now click on ... on the top to open the actions menu.

Click on Authorize first to authorize the payment.

On linked2pay portal, please go to Card section under Reporting to see the payment is authorized and ready to be captured.

Finally click on Capture to capture the authorized payment.

On linked2pay portal, you should be able to see the payment is captured.
