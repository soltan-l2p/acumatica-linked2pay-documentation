Recurring Payments

Pay with AutoPayBill

Pay the generated recurring invoice with AutoPayBill and the saved customer method.

In Receivables under Processes you will find the AutoPayBill section. Open it...

(Screen ID: AR508800)

...in the Auto Bill page, Set the Invoices Due Before date to the date you wanna pay until.
(for example I'll set till 1 September)

Note: If you have multiple invoices due for that date, page will show all of them at once.

Either:

  • select the invoice you want to pay and click on Process.
  • Or click on Process All to pay all invoices due for that date.

In this example we've picked all of those recurring transactions which we just generated and clicked on Process button.

This one may take a little time since it bulk processes all the transactions in once.

And we processed all of the generated recurring transactions by linked2pay.
