Customers 1

Create Customer

Start in Acumatica by creating or opening the customer record that will be used for the rest of the linked2pay demo.

Customer record

Create or select the customer first.

Walk the viewer through the customer identity fields, account settings, and billing contact details. This is the foundation for everything that happens later in linked2pay.

Review details

Pause on the fields that matter.

Point out the customer name, status, address, billing contact, and any required account settings. The demo should feel calm here, because this screen gives the viewer orientation.

Ready for next step

Confirm the customer is ready.

Close the step by confirming that the customer has enough information to be boarded from Acumatica to linked2pay.

At the end of this step, the viewer should understand which customer record will be used and why it is ready for the linked2pay workflow.
