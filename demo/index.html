Demo Walkthrough

Follow the demo path in order: start with customers and payment methods, move into invoice and recurring payment flows, then finish with linked2pay Center.

Demo Flow

Customers, payment methods, invoices, recurring payments, then linked2pay Center.

The demo is split into clear sections so the viewer always knows what kind of work they are watching. Start with the customer, prepare payment methods, pay invoices, repeat the same idea through recurring payments, and finish with linked2pay Center tools.

Use this guided path to demonstrate each documented workflow with training data. Validate production behavior against the testing matrix before go-live.

01 Create Customer Begin with the customer record used throughout the demo. 02 Board Customer Move the customer from Acumatica into linked2pay. 03 Sync Customer Confirm the customer stays aligned with linked2pay. 04 Create Payment Methods Add test payment methods in the linked2pay sandbox. 05 Sync Customer with linked2pay Bring payment method details back into Acumatica. 06 Payment Method CRUD Show what changes after updates or deletions. 07 Defaults and Cash Account Set the recommended defaults for payment work. 08 Create Invoice Create the invoice that will be paid in the demo. 09 Pay with Card Pay the invoice using a saved card method. 10 Pay with ACH Pay the invoice using a saved ACH method. 11 Pay with AutoPayBill Use AutoPayBill for the invoice payment. 12 Create Invoice (Template) Create the recurring invoice template. 13 Add to Schedule Add the template to the recurring schedule. 14 Generate Invoices Generate invoices from the recurring schedule. 15 Pay with AutoPayBill Pay the generated recurring invoice. 16 Bulk Sync Customers Use linked2pay Center to sync multiple customers.
