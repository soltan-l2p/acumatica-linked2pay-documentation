linked2pay for Acumatica

Demo Walkthrough

Follow the original demo path in order: start with customer creation and synchronization, then move into payment methods, payment method changes, defaults, and cash account setup.

Demo Flow

Customers first, then payment methods.

The demo is split into two sections so the viewer always knows what kind of work they are watching. The Customers section shows how the customer record gets prepared and synchronized. The Payment Methods section shows how linked2pay methods are created, synced, changed, deleted, and tied back to Acumatica defaults.

Treat the text here as a walking guide. The screenshots can be replaced later with final production screenshots while keeping the same route and sidebar structure.

01 Create Customer Begin in Acumatica with the customer record that will be used throughout the demo. 02 Board Customer Show how the customer moves from Acumatica into linked2pay. 03 Sync Customer Confirm that the customer stays aligned between Acumatica and linked2pay. 04 Create Payment Methods Add sample payment methods in linked2pay for the customer. 05 Sync Customer with linked2pay Bring linked2pay customer payment method details back into the Acumatica flow. 06 Payment Method CRUD Demonstrate what changes after payment methods are updated or deleted. 07 Defaults and Cash Account Set the recommended default payment method and cash account values.
