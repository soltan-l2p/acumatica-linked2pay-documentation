Customer Payment Profile Demo

Use this sample page to show how a user would review a customer record before taking a payment-related action with linked2pay.

Customer record

Start from the customer you want to review.

Open the customer record in Acumatica and pause on the fields that matter for the demo: customer name, account status, billing contact, and any linked2pay-related profile details. This gives the viewer the context before you move into payment activity.

Next, point out where saved payment information or linked2pay sync status would appear. The goal is not to rush the person through the screen. Let them see which customer is being used and why this record is ready for payment activity.

Finish the customer portion by confirming what the viewer should remember: the customer is selected, the payment profile is available, and the next step can move into a transaction workflow.
