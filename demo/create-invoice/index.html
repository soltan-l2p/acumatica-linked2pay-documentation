Invoice

Create Invoice

Create a sample invoice in Acumatica and prepare it for the linked2pay payment flow.

Invoices page

Open the invoice workspace

Go to Receivables, under Transactions click on Invoices and Memos

(Screen ID: AR3010PL).

Click the + button to create a new invoice record.

You should face the invoice form where you can fill in the invoice details, line items, and amounts.

Pick your customer and fill in the invoice details, line items, and etc.

When you are done, click the Save button on the top and then click on the Release.

This page demonstrates how to create an invoice in Acumatica and prepare it for the linked2pay payment flow. Make sure to fill in all necessary details and release the invoice to proceed with payment processing.
