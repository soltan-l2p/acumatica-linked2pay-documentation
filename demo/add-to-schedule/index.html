Recurring Payments

Add to Schedule

Add the invoice template to a recurring schedule so invoices can be generated later.

Click on ... and then under Other you will see "Add to Schedule" button.

Click on that, it will bring you to Recurring Transactions page (Screen ID: AR203500).

Fill the required fields and click on Save button.

For this example:

  • Description: Subscription Recurring Transaction
  • Execution Limit: 10 times
  • Recurrence: Every 2 days
  • Invoice: It should be already populated

Go to Receivables, under Processes click on Generate Recurring Transactions.

(Screen ID: AR504000).

Now you should be ready to go ahead and generate recurring invoices.
