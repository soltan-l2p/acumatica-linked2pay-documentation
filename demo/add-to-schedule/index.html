Recurring Billing

Add the Invoice Template to a Schedule

Tell Acumatica when and how often to create invoices from the recurring template.

Open Recurring Transactions

From the invoice template, open the Actions menu and choose Add to Schedule. Acumatica opens Recurring Transactions (AR203500).

Define the billing pattern

Enter the description, start date, recurrence, and optional execution limit. For a subscription demonstration, use a short test interval; for production, use the customer's approved monthly or other billing cycle.

Keep the two schedules distinct

This recurring transaction schedule defines what invoices are due. The separate Acumatica automation schedule defines when the Generate Recurring Transactions process runs unattended.

The recurring template and schedule create invoices. linked2pay Auto-Bill later creates and captures the payments.
