Payment Methods

Sync Customer with linked2pay

Return to Acumatica and sync the customer so the linked2pay payment methods become visible in the customer workflow.

Return to Acumatica

Go to Acumatica and Synchronize.

In Acumatica Customer page, under the linked2pay Integration tab, click on the Sync with linked2pay button to synchronize the payment methods you created in linked2pay.

Under Payment Methods tab, you should see the payment methods you created in linked2pay now available in Acumatica.

RECOMMENDED: Set one of those payment methods as the default for the customer in Acumatica by checking the "Is Default" checkbox.

Then click on the payment method and then click on the "View Payment Method" button.

By default Cash Account may be empty but you have to select the Cash Account that you want to use for this payment method.

Set your cash account and save it.

IMPORTANT: Do this step for each payment method you synced from linked2pay.

The viewer should now see the bridge between linked2pay payment methods and the Acumatica customer record.
