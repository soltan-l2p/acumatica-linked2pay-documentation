Payment Methods

Sync Customer with linked2pay

Return to Acumatica and sync the customer so the linked2pay payment methods become visible in the customer workflow.

Return to Acumatica

Go to Acumatica and Synchronize.

In Acumatica Customer page, under the linked2pay Integration tab, click on the Sync with linked2pay button to synchronize the payment methods you created in linked2pay.

Under Payment Methods tab, you should see the payment methods you created in linked2pay now available in Acumatica.

This page is where the demo can prove that changes in linked2pay are reflected clearly in Acumatica after sync.
