Payment Methods

Sync Wallet Payment Methods

Mirror linked2pay cards and bank accounts into Acumatica using each wallet item's Payment Profile ID.

Create wallet methods in linked2pay

Add one or more cards or bank accounts to the connected contact. linked2pay assigns a unique Payment Profile ID to every card and bank account.

Sync the customer in Acumatica

On the customer, click Sync with linked2pay. Acumatica creates new profiles only for Payment Profile IDs it does not already have and updates matching profiles instead of duplicating them.

Acumatica stores only token identifiers and masked display values. Full card and bank account numbers are never copied into the customer payment method.
