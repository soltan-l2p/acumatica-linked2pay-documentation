Bulk Sync Customers

Use linked2pay Center to sync multiple customer records from Acumatica into linked2pay in one guided step.

We already synced customers one by one.

In the previous steps, we experienced syncing customers one by one from Acumatica to linked2pay. If you want to review that flow again, open the Sync Customer with linked2pay page.

But what if you have multiple customers and you want to sync and board all of them together? That is where linked2pay Center becomes useful.

Get Started with Bulk Sync

In Receivables, under Processes, click on linked2pay Center.

You will see the list of Customers you have in Acumatica.

Each customer has an L2P Contact Profile ID and Boarded to External Gateway status. These fields are currently empty because the customers have not been synced yet.

Use the pagination controls in the bottom-right corner to navigate through the customer list, or use the search bar in the top-right corner to quickly find a specific customer.

You can also sort and filter the list by L2P Contact Profile ID and Boarded to External Gateway.

Either:

  • Select the customers you want to sync and click on " Sync Selected Customers to linked2pay"
  • or you can simply click "Sync All Customers to linked2pay " to sync all customers.

117 out of 121 Customers have been successfully synced with linked2pay.

Note: The failed ones were because of invalid address information. Make sure your customers have valid informaiton.

Result

You should be able to see those customers in your linked2pay portal under My Contacts sections.
