linked2pay Center

Bulk Sync Customers

Create, update, recover, and mirror multiple connected customers while keeping row-level results.

Open linked2pay Center

Go to Receivables > Processes > linked2pay Center (LP102000). Set Operation to Sync Customers with linked2pay.

Choose the scope

Filter or search the customer list. Select specific rows and use Sync Selected Customers to linked2pay, or use Sync All Customers to linked2pay for the complete list.

Let each customer follow the correct path

  • No Contact Profile ID: create the contact in linked2pay.
  • Existing contact: update customer data and mirror the wallet.
  • Stored ID no longer found: recreate the contact, save the new ID, and deactivate stale profiles.

Review the progress report

The Acumatica processing window shows processed, successful, warning, and failed rows. A failure is recorded without stopping other customers. Correct the row's data or setup, then rerun it.

Verify linked2pay

Open My Contacts in linked2pay and confirm the successful customers. In Acumatica, verify their read-only Contact Profile IDs and mirrored payment methods.

While the sync is running, do not close the window or start overlapping customer operations.
