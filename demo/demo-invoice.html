Invoice review

Begin with the invoice the customer wants to pay.

Show the invoice number, customer, open balance, and due date first. This helps the viewer understand the transaction before you introduce linked2pay into the flow.

Next, guide the viewer to the payment action. This is where the real demo can show whether the user is collecting a card payment, sending a payment link, or using another linked2pay-supported payment option.
