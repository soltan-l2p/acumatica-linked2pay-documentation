Payment Methods

Create Payment Methods in linked2pay

Move into linked2pay and create payment methods for later Acumatica use.

linked2pay Portal

Go to payment methods tab.

In the linked2pay portal, navigate to the payment methods tab for the customer you created in the previous step.

Click the "Add New Card".

Either create a credit card payment method...

or create an ACH payment method...

If you did at least one of the above, you should see a saved method(s).

At the end of this step, you should have at least one payment method created in linked2pay for the customer you created in Acumatica.
