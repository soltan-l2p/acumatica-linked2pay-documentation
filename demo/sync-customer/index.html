Sync Customer with linked2pay

Demonstrate the sync moment that keeps customer information aligned between Acumatica and linked2pay.

First update customer in Acumatica

As you can see in the screenshot, I've updated the customer information in Acumatica for simulation purposes.

Now go to linked2pay Integration tab and click on the Sync with linked2pay button to sync the customer information from Acumatica to linked2pay.

linked2pay Portal

After the sync, you can see the updated customer information in linked2pay portal.

At the end of this step, you should have a customer record in linked2pay that is synced with the latest information from Acumatica.
