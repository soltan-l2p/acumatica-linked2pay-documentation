Customers

Update and Sync a Customer

Send current Acumatica customer information to the existing linked2pay contact and refresh its wallet.

Update the Acumatica customer

Change the customer information you want to demonstrate, then save the record. The integration checks the primary contact email and the account contact email and uses the available valid value.

Run Sync with linked2pay

Open the linked2pay Integration tab and click Sync with linked2pay. With a valid Contact Profile ID, the integration updates the existing contact and mirrors its card and bank profiles into Acumatica.

Recovery when the portal contact was deleted

If the stored Contact Profile ID no longer exists in linked2pay, Sync recreates the contact, stores the new read-only ID, and deactivates stale linked2pay payment methods in Acumatica. The user does not need a separate Board action.

Verify the result

Confirm the linked2pay contact reflects the latest Acumatica data. The success message reports customer and payment-method changes.

The Contact Profile ID is the connection key. It is populated and maintained by the integration, not manually edited by users.
