Payment Methods

Demonstrate CRUD of Payment Methods

Show what happens when linked2pay payment methods are created, changed, deleted, and then synchronized back into Acumatica.

Add new methods

Add a new payment method.

Starting by the creation of new payment methods in linked2pay.

Now synchronize it in Acumatica.

Click on the "Sync with linked2pay" button in Acumatica to see the new payment method appear in the list.

As you can see, the new payment methods is now available in Acumatica after the sync. You can also demonstrate updating an existing payment method and then syncing again to show the changes reflected in Acumatica.

Delete some and synchronize in Acumatica.

You should be able to see the deleted payment methods are de-activated and marked as (deleted) in Acumatica after the sync.

The reason it doesn't delete it completely is because Acumatica needs to keep a record of the payment method for historical transactions.

Important: If you delete a payment method in linked2pay that is default for a customer in Acumatica, you will need to set a new default payment method for that customer in Acumatica before you sync.

This page is a demonstration of the create, update, and delete behavior of linked2pay payment methods and how they are reflected in Acumatica after synchronization. Make sure to follow the steps carefully to understand the process and implications of managing payment methods across both platforms.
