Invoice

Pay with linked2pay Auto-Bill

Process all eligible due AR invoices with each customer's active default linked2pay payment profile.

Prepare an eligible invoice

Create and release an AR invoice with a due date on or before the test date. The invoice can be entered manually or generated from a recurring schedule.

Verify the customer

The customer must have Enable Auto Bill checked and an active default linked2pay card or ACH profile with Contact Profile ID, Payment Profile ID, processing center, and cash account.

Open linked2pay Auto-Bill

Go to Receivables > Processes > linked2pay Auto-Bill (AR508800). Set Invoices Due Before to the test date. The list includes every eligible due invoice, not only recurring invoices.

Process and review results

Select rows and click Process, or click Process All. The processing window reports each invoice separately. Repeated or overlapping runs must not capture an already approved payment twice.

Recover a captured but unreleased payment

If linked2pay capture succeeds but Acumatica release fails, open Captured But Unreleased. Correct the release problem, then use Retry Captured Payment Releases. This releases the existing payment without charging the customer again.

For production, schedule Auto-Bill after Acumatica's recurring invoice generation. The manual Process action is useful for demonstrations and controlled testing.
