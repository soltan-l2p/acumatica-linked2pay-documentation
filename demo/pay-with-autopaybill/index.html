Invoice

Pay with AutoPayBill

Use AutoPayBill to pay the invoice with the recommended saved payment method.

Create Invoice

Create a brand new invoice in Acumatica and save it. Make sure you set the due date for today (the day you are implementing the demo walkthrough).

Make sure your customer has been enabled for AutoPayBill and has a default payment method set.

If the customer has NOT been enabled for AutoPayBill, you will NOT see his invoice listed under the AutoPayBill section.

Note: Check Enable AutoPayBill for more details on how to enable AutoPayBill for a customer.

In Receivables under Processes you will find the AutoPayBill section. Open it...

(Screen ID: AR508800)

...in the Auto Bill page, Set the Invoices Due Before date to today (the day you are implementing the demo walkthrough).

Note: If you have multiple invoices due for that date, page will show all of them at once.

Either:

  • select the invoice you want to pay and click on Process.
  • Or click on Process All to pay all invoices due for that date.

You will see the loading screen with real time progress of the payment processing. Once the payment is completed, you will see the confirmation message on the screen. The invoice is now paid with AutoPayBill using the default payment method set for the customer. You can also check the linked2pay portal to see the payment status of the invoice.

AutoPayBill should feel like the shortest path once the customer and payment defaults are already prepared.
