Setup Step 3

Create or Confirm Payment Methods

Finally, confirm the payment methods Acumatica will use when Linked2Pay processes credit card and ACH payments.

Now we prepare the payment methods

Let's make sure Acumatica has the right payment methods.

In Acumatica, go to Banking > Preferences > Payment Methods (Screen ID: CA2040PL).

This is where you confirm the payment methods that Linked2Pay will use for credit card and ACH payments. At the end of this step, you should have one method for credit cards and one method for ACH/bank payments.

Credit card payment method

In payment methods page click + (New Record) to create a new payment method.

Fill the values for the credit card payment method.

  • Payment Method ID: L2PCC
  • Active: Checked
  • Means of Payment: Credit Card
  • Description: Linked2Pay Credit Card Payments

And please make sure you check:

  • Use in AP: Checked
  • Use in AR: Checked
  • Require Remittance Information for Cash Account: Checked

Then make sure you add your Cash Account to the payment method. This is the account that will receive the credit card payments.

  • Use in AP: Checked
  • Use in AR: Checked

Then go the Processing Center tab and add the Linked2Pay processing center to the payment method.

This is the processing center that will handle the credit card payments.

Click on Search button and from the selections pick L2PCC (This is the processing center for Credit Cards we just created) and click on Select.

It should look like this.

Finally you have to setup "SETTINGS FOR USE IN AR". You just have to simply add new Payment Method Details.

  • ID: CCPID
  • Description: Payment Profile ID
  • Required: checked
  • Payment Profile ID: checked
  • Sort Order: 1
Click on Save button on the top and you are done with the credit card payment method.
Now we will move to the ACH/bank payment method.

ACH payment method

For the ACH/bank payment method, follow the same steps used for the credit card payment method. The only differences are listed below:

  • Payment Method ID: L2PACH
  • Means of Payment: Credit Card (it is not a mistake, we still pick Credit Card even for ACH payments)
  • Description: Linked2Pay ACH/Bank Payments
So we are basically done confirming the payment methods. The extension is published, the processing center is ready, and Acumatica has the payment methods it needs for the next workflow.
