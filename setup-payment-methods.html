Setup 3A

Create the Credit Card Payment Method

Create and configure the Acumatica payment method used for linked2pay credit card payments.

Payment Methods

Prepare the credit card payment method first.

In Acumatica, go to Banking > Preferences > Payment Methods (Screen ID: CA2040PL).

This screen is where you confirm the payment methods linked2pay will use for credit card and ACH payments.

Credit card payment method

On the Payment Methods page, click + (New Record) to create a new payment method.

Enter the values for the credit card payment method:

  • Payment Method ID: L2PCC
  • Active: Checked
  • Means of Payment: Credit Card
  • Description: linked2pay Credit Card Payments

Also confirm the following options are selected:

  • Use in AP: Checked
  • Use in AR: Checked
  • Require Remittance Information for Cash Account: Checked

Add your cash account to the payment method. This is the account that will receive credit card payments.

  • Use in AP: Checked
  • Use in AR: Checked
