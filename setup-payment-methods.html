Setup Step 2

Create Or Confirm Payment Methods

Linked2Pay uses Acumatica payment methods to know whether a payment is a credit card or ACH/bank payment.

Where To Go

Open this screen first.

In Acumatica, go to Banking > Preferences > Payment Methods.

You should have payment methods like L2PCC for Linked2Pay credit card payments and L2PACH for Linked2Pay ACH/bank payments.

Make sure these payment methods are active and available for Accounts Receivable.
