Why linked2pay

Built for the people who own cash flow, not just the people who configure ERPs

⚡

Get paid faster

Customers pay invoices by card, ACH, or AutoPayBill the moment they're due - shrinking days sales outstanding and improving cash flow.

💰

Reduce processing costs

Built-in processing fee programs let you offset card costs and steer customers toward low-cost ACH - fees are calculated and applied automatically on each payment.

🔄

Automate reconciliation

Payments post to the right invoice, cash account, and AR period automatically. No exporting, re-keying, or end-of-month matching marathons.

👥

Eliminate double entry

Customers and saved payment methods sync both ways between Acumatica and linked2pay - one record, always current, on both sides.

📅

Recurring billing on autopilot

Turn invoices into templates, add them to a schedule, and let AutoPayBill generate and collect recurring invoices without manual touches.

🔒

Secure by design

Card and bank details are tokenized and vaulted with linked2pay - sensitive payment data never lives in your ERP, keeping your Acumatica instance out of PCI scope.
