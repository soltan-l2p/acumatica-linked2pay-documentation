Why linked2pay?

More than just an ERP integration,
it's built to improve cash flow.

FAST

Get paid faster

Customers pay invoices by card, ACH, or AutoPayBill the moment they're due - shrinking days sales outstanding and improving cash flow.

SAVE

Reduce processing costs

Built-in processing fee programs let you offset card costs and steer customers toward low-cost ACH - fees are calculated and applied automatically on each payment.

SYNC

Simplify reconciliation

The integration applies payments to Acumatica documents and tracks gateway status. Finance validates cash accounts, settlement, returns, and generated batches during reconciliation.

2-WAY

Eliminate double entry

Customer records can be boarded from Acumatica to linked2pay, while supported payment-method and transaction updates synchronize back to Acumatica.

AUTO

Recurring billing on autopilot

Turn invoices into templates, add them to a schedule, and let AutoPayBill generate and collect recurring invoices without manual touches.

PCI

Secure by design

Card and bank details are tokenized and vaulted with linked2pay, reducing sensitive-data exposure in Acumatica. Tokenization does not remove the merchant's PCI responsibilities.
