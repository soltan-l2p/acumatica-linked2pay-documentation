Executive Summary

One integration, focused around payment operations.

Customer Boarding Payment Method Sync Auto-Bill Credit Card Fee Webhook Updates
01

Connect the customer

Acumatica stores the Linked2Pay Contact ID so a customer can be boarded, synced, and connected to saved payment profiles.

02

Sync the wallet

Credit card and ACH profiles are represented in Acumatica with masked values and Linked2Pay Payment Profile IDs.

03

Automate collections

Auto-Bill can process eligible due invoices using the customer's saved Linked2Pay payment method.

Important: This is an Acumatica customization extension. It does not modify Acumatica source code.
