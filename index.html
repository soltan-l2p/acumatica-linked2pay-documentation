Questions buyers ask first

Frequently asked questions

What exactly is linked2pay for Acumatica?

It's a payment processing extension that installs directly into your Acumatica ERP. It lets your AR team accept credit card and ACH payments on invoices, manage saved payment methods, run recurring billing, and reconcile automatically — all from the Acumatica screens they already use.

Does it support both credit cards and ACH?

Yes. Both are first-class payment methods with their own processing centers, saved (tokenized) accounts on the customer record, and full support in one-time and recurring payments.

Do payments sync back into Acumatica automatically?

Yes. Payments apply to the correct invoices, cash accounts, and AR periods automatically, and a webhook connection keeps payment statuses current — no manual posting or spreadsheet matching.

Can it handle recurring invoices and auto-collection?

Yes. Create an invoice template, add it to a recurring schedule, and AutoPayBill generates and collects the invoices automatically against the customer's saved payment method.

How long does implementation take?

Setup is a guided, no-code process: import the customization project, connect your linked2pay account, and configure two payment methods. Most teams complete it in days — the step-by-step setup guide covers every screen.

Will it reduce our processing costs?

Two ways: built-in processing fee programs can offset card acceptance costs automatically, and making ACH as easy as card shifts volume to the lowest-cost rail.

Is it secure? Where is card data stored?

Card and bank account details are tokenized and vaulted with linked2pay's PCI-compliant platform. Your Acumatica instance only ever stores tokens — sensitive payment data stays out of your ERP.
