Customers

In Acumatica, customers can be synchronized with linked2pay, where they are created and managed as Contacts. This allows both systems to share customer information and maintain consistency.

Customer details, saved payment methods, and other related information can be synchronized between Acumatica and linked2pay. This ensures that payment data is readily available when processing transactions.

In addition to syncing individual customers, Acumatica also supports bulk synchronization, making it easy to import or update multiple customer records in linked2pay at once.
