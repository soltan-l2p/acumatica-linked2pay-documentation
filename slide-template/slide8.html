Recurring Transactions

Acumatica can generate recurring transactions based on predefined schedules, making it easy to automate recurring invoices for subscriptions, memberships, and other regularly billed services.

Once recurring transactions are generated, linked2pay can automatically process the associated payments using the customer's saved payment method. This eliminates the need for manual payment collection while ensuring transactions are processed on schedule.

In this demonstration, we'll generate recurring transactions in Acumatica and see how linked2pay automatically processes the payments and keeps both systems synchronized.
