Support Notes

Before Going Live

A successful launch depends on clean setup, validated credentials, and a tested customer and payment workflow.

Guided Walkthrough

Close with a clean go-live check.

This page is the final pass before launch. It helps the reader confirm credentials, customers, wallet sync, automation, and webhook setup.

Treat this like a calm checklist. By the end, the user should know exactly what is ready and what still needs attention.

Area Confirm
Processing Center Linked2Pay processing center is active and credentials pass testing.
Payment Methods L2PCC and/or L2PACH are active and attached to the processing center.
Customers Customers have Linked2Pay Contact IDs and correct Auto-Bill settings.
Wallet Payment methods are synced and show the correct Payment Profile IDs.
Automation Recurring transaction generation and Linked2Pay Auto-Bill schedules are configured if automation is required.
Webhook Webhook URL and API key validation are configured for status updates.

Linked2Pay for Acumatica is designed to make customer payments easier, safer, and more automated while keeping Acumatica as the source of accounting truth.
