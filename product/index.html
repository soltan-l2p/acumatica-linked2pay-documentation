Product tour

It looks like Acumatica, because it lives in Acumatica

No separate portal to babysit. Your AR team works the screens they already know - linked2pay adds the payment muscle behind them. Every screenshot below is the real product.

Customers & payment methods, synced both ways

Board a customer from Acumatica to linked2pay in one click. Saved cards and bank accounts appear on the customer record's own linked2pay Integration and Payment Methods tabs - created, updated, and removed in lockstep on both platforms.

See the sync walkthrough →

Pay any invoice by card - with fees handled automatically

Apply a payment on the standard Acumatica payment screen using a saved, tokenized linked2pay card. The processing fee is calculated and applied per payment, and the amount posts straight to the open invoice.

See a card payment →

ACH payments, native and low-cost

The same flow works for bank payments: a saved, tokenized ACH account on the customer record pays the invoice directly from the Acumatica payment screen - shifting volume to the lowest-cost rail.

See an ACH payment →

Recurring invoices that collect themselves

Generate invoices from recurring schedules and let AutoPayBill process them automatically - this batch finished in 15 seconds with zero errors. Bulk tools in linked2pay Center handle whole customer lists at once.
