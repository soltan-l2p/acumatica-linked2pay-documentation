Presenter Notes

Guide the room through the why before the clicks.

Start by reminding the viewer that Acumatica is still the accounting home base. Then show how Linked2Pay quietly handles the payment profile and transaction side.

When you replace the placeholder image, use the screen you want people to remember most: the processing center, customer integration tab, or successful payment record.

1

Set the context

Explain the manual pain first: repeated collections, stored payment references, and status follow-up.

2

Show the customer

Open the customer record and point out the Linked2Pay Contact ID and Auto-Bill setting.

3

Show the wallet

Walk through masked card or ACH profiles and explain why sensitive numbers stay in Linked2Pay.

4

Finish with the result

Run or describe Auto-Bill, then show the payment record and status update as the payoff.
