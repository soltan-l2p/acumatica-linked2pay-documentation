How The Integration Works

Acumatica remains the accounting system of record. Linked2Pay handles gateway-side contact profiles, payment profiles, and transaction processing.

Keep the architecture simple.

Use this page when someone asks where each record lives. Acumatica stays the accounting source of truth, and Linked2Pay stays responsible for gateway-side payment profiles and transactions.
